Heavy psychedelic guitar & drums duo from Austin, Texas

For fans of Electric Wizard, Uncle Acid and the Deadbeats, early My Bloody Valentine

Biography

Floating Cube is a heavy psychedelic guitar and drums duo from Austin, TX. Jake Stuart (baritone guitar) and Carissa Giard (drums) met in the music program at Florida Atlantic University in 2012. After playing in many other projects (Peyote Coyote, The Stargazer Lilies, Sailor Poon), they decided to start their own creative endeavor inspired by a wide range of influences, from shoegaze and metal to surf rock and jazz. Blending dark, heavy riffs into pastel shoegazey soundscapes, Floating Cube swirls together an immersive and hypnotic wall of sound that feels sonically massive for a duo.

The band will be releasing their debut self-titled EP on July 25 with a digital release, 7” records, cassette tapes, handmade band-branded guitar pedals, and a release party to follow.
